EVERSHIELD INSURANCE LTD.
INTERNAL OPERATIONS PROCEDURES MANUAL
Version 3.2
Effective Date: 01 January 2026

CONFIDENTIAL - INTERNAL USE ONLY

==================================================
1. PURPOSE
==================================================

This document outlines the standard operating procedures for employees of EverShield Insurance Ltd. The objective is to ensure consistent handling of customer policies, claims, underwriting activities, and data management.

==================================================
2. NEW POLICY ISSUANCE PROCEDURE
==================================================

1. Receive customer application through approved channels.
2. Verify customer identity documentation.
3. Review risk assessment questionnaire.
4. Conduct underwriting review.
5. Approve, decline, or request additional information.
6. Generate policy documentation.
7. Collect initial premium payment.
8. Activate policy in the Policy Management System.
9. Send policy package to customer.

Target completion time: 2 business days.

==================================================
3. CLAIMS HANDLING PROCEDURE
==================================================

3.1 Claim Registration

1. Receive claim notification.
2. Create claim record in Claims Management System.
3. Assign unique claim number.
4. Acknowledge receipt to customer.

3.2 Investigation

1. Review policy coverage.
2. Verify policy status.
3. Collect supporting evidence.
4. Request additional documentation if necessary.
5. Assign adjuster when required.

3.3 Settlement

1. Approve or deny claim.
2. Document decision rationale.
3. Obtain supervisor approval for settlements above GHS 50,000.
4. Process payment.
5. Close claim file.

==================================================
4. CUSTOMER DATA HANDLING
==================================================

- Customer information must remain confidential.
- Access is limited to authorized personnel.
- Employee passwords must not be shared.
- Customer records must be retained according to company policy.
- Any suspected data breach must be reported immediately.

==================================================
5. UNDERWRITING GUIDELINES
==================================================

Risk factors considered:

- Claims history
- Driving history
- Vehicle value
- Geographic risk
- Fraud indicators

Escalate applications that exceed risk thresholds to a Senior Underwriter.

==================================================
6. FRAUD PREVENTION
==================================================

Employees must report suspicious activities including:

- Inconsistent documentation
- Altered records
- Identity discrepancies
- Repeated high-value claims

Potential fraud cases shall be referred to the Special Investigations Unit.

==================================================
7. CUSTOMER COMPLAINTS
==================================================

1. Log complaint.
2. Acknowledge within 24 hours.
3. Investigate complaint.
4. Respond within 10 business days.
5. Escalate unresolved complaints to management.

==================================================
8. BUSINESS CONTINUITY
==================================================

In the event of system outages:

1. Switch to approved manual processing procedures.
2. Record transactions using contingency forms.
3. Notify IT department immediately.
4. Reconcile records after system restoration.

==================================================
9. APPROVAL AUTHORITY MATRIX
==================================================

Customer Service Agent:
- Policy updates
- Basic inquiries

Claims Officer:
- Claims up to GHS 20,000

Claims Manager:
- Claims up to GHS 100,000

Executive Management:
- Claims above GHS 100,000
- Exceptional approvals

==================================================
10. DOCUMENT CONTROL
==================================================

Owner: Operations Department
Review Frequency: Annual
